About LED Unlimited Lighting

Illuminating Spaces Since 2010

Our Story

Established in 2010, LED Unlimited Lighting has grown to become a leading provider of energy-efficient lighting solutions in Florida. From our headquarters in Oakland Park, we serve businesses and homeowners with state-of-the-art LED lighting products and professional installation services.

Our commitment to environmental preservation drives us to provide sustainable lighting solutions that reduce energy consumption while delivering superior illumination.

Commercial Solutions

Residential Lighting

Energy Consulting

Expert advice on energy-efficient lighting solutions and cost savings

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring the future of green lighting to every corner of the world, promoting environmental preservation through energy-efficient LED solutions. We strive to be the leading one-stop lighting solution provider, offering innovative products and exceptional service.

Established in 2010, LED Unlimited Lighting brings sustainable and energy-efficient lighting solutions to businesses and homes across Florida and beyond.
