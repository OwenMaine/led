About Us

Established in 2010, LED Unlimited Lighting Inc. has been at the forefront of green lighting solutions in Florida and beyond. From our headquarters at 4257 NE 6th Ave, Oakland Park, FL, we've maintained our vision of bringing sustainable LED lighting to every corner of the world.

As a specialized wholesale distributor, we offer a comprehensive range of LED products including indoor and outdoor lighting, LED tubes, bulb lights, spotlights, downlights, flood lights, and high bay solutions. Our commitment to environmental preservation drives us to provide energy-efficient lighting solutions that make a difference.
